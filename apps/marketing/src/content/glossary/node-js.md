Characterized by its event-driven, non-blocking input/output model, Node.js is particularly suited for applications requiring high throughput and low latency, such as real-time messaging, streaming services, or microservices architectures. It has fostered a vibrant ecosystem, with numerous libraries and frameworks, and a community that continuously advances the technology. The platform’s adaptability across various operating systems, including Windows, Linux, and macOS, further expands its reach.
