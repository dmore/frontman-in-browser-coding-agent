It functions as the blueprint for structuring, building, and maintaining web applications, ensuring that codebases remain scalable, maintainable, and adaptable over time. This concept addresses concerns such as modularity, separation of concerns, performance optimization, and integration with backend services. As web applications grow increasingly complex, a robust architectural approach enables teams to manage dependencies, streamline workflows, and foster efficient collaboration. By implementing well-considered patterns and principles, frontend architecture facilitates the delivery of consistent experiences across devices and platforms.
