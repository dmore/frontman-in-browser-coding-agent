Its primary purpose is to deliver concise, authoritative responses directly within the search interface, often above traditional organic listings. The content is programmatically extracted from a webpage and presented in formats such as paragraphs, lists, or tables, depending on the nature of the query. Search engines select and display these snippets based on relevance and content quality, aiming to enhance user experience by offering rapid access to essential information. The growing importance of Featured Snippets is evident by their prevalence in high-intent search scenarios and their impact on click-through rates.
