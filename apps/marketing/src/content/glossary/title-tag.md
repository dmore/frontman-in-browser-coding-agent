Positioned within the <code>&lt;head&gt;</code> section of an HTML document, it shapes the text displayed in browser tabs and search engine result pages (SERPs). When search engines crawl a site, the title tag acts as a concise summary of a page's content, affecting both user experience and search engine optimization (SEO) outcomes. Its influence extends beyond the browser, impacting how a page is presented on social media and influencing click-through rates. A well-crafted title tag follows best practices in length and keyword placement, ensuring clarity and relevance. As an integral part of on-page SEO, its optimization can boost discoverability and page ranking.
