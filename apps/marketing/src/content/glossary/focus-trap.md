Rather than allowing users to inadvertently tab out of an active component, the mechanism cycles focus through the focusable elements within a designated boundary. A focus trap typically intercepts keyboard events like Tab and Shift+Tab, redirecting the user’s attention to stay within the appropriate context. This method supports the delivery of a seamless experience by preventing background interactions that could disrupt workflows or lead to confusion.
