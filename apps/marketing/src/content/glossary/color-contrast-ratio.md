<h2>What is Color Contrast Ratio?</h2><p>Color Contrast Ratio represents a numerical expression of the luminance difference between two colors, typically foreground and background, on a digital interface. This metric quantifies how distinguishable text, icons, or graphical elements are from their background, measured on a scale from 1:1 (no contrast) to 21:1 (maximum contrast). The calculation is based on relative luminance values, ensuring that visual information is accessible to a broader audience, including individuals with various visual impairments. Recognized as a pivotal aspect of web and interface accessibility, the ratio directly affects readability, user engagement, and compliance with standards such as WCAG. As digital environments grow increasingly complex, attention to these ratios becomes essential for usability and inclusivity.
