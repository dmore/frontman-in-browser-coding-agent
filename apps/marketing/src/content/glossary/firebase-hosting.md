It enables developers to serve HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and other assets, leveraging a global content delivery network (CDN) to ensure low latency for users worldwide. The platform caters to both simple landing pages and complex single-page applications, supporting modern web development practices. By streamlining deployment through command-line tools and deep integration with development workflows, Firebase Hosting simplifies the process of taking projects from local environments to live production.
