<h2>What is CSS Subgrid?</h2><p>CSS Subgrid is a feature designed to enhance the capabilities of CSS Grid by allowing nested grid containers—known as subgrids—to inherit and align with the grid lines of their parent grid. Unlike conventional nested grids, where each child grid establishes its own independent track sizing, subgrids enable direct participation in the parent’s column or row structure. This results in layouts where alignment across deeply nested elements is precise and consistent. The primary aim of subgrid is to address the limitations of traditional grid layouts, particularly when managing complex hierarchies. By synchronizing track sizing between parent and child containers, developers can achieve cohesive designs without resorting to convoluted workarounds or excessive code.
