# Frontman Trademark Policy

**Version 1.0 — Effective 2026-04-07**

## 1. Purpose

This policy describes the permitted and prohibited uses of the Frontman trademarks. Our goal is to encourage widespread use and adoption of Frontman while ensuring that the trademarks remain reliable indicators of quality and origin.

## 2. Protected Marks

The following trademarks are owned by Frontman AI:

- **"Frontman"** — wordmark
- **"Frontman AI"** — company name
- **Frontman logo** — any graphical logo used by Frontman AI to represent the project
- **frontman.ai** — domain name

These marks may or may not be registered in any jurisdiction. This policy applies regardless of registration status.

## 3. Permitted Uses

The following uses of our marks are permitted without prior written approval:

### 3.1 Nominative (Referential) Use

You may use the Frontman name to truthfully refer to the Frontman project or Frontman AI:

- In blog posts, articles, talks, academic papers, and social media discussing Frontman
- In comparative analysis or reviews
- Linking to the official Frontman website or repository

### 3.2 Attribution

You may use the Frontman name for attribution purposes:

- "Built with Frontman"
- "Powered by Frontman"
- "Compatible with Frontman"
- "Based on Frontman" (for forks — see Section 5)

### 3.3 Community Use

You may use the Frontman name in the context of community activities:

- User groups, meetups, and conferences (e.g., "Frontman Users Meetup")
- Educational materials and tutorials

In all permitted uses, you must:
- Not alter, abbreviate, or combine the marks with other trademarks
- Not use the marks in a way that suggests endorsement or affiliation with Frontman AI, unless you have a written agreement with us
- Include a notice that "Frontman is a trademark of Frontman AI" where reasonable

## 4. Prohibited Uses

The following uses are **not permitted** without prior written approval from Frontman AI:

### 4.1 Commercial Product Naming

- Naming any product, service, company, or organization "Frontman," "Frontman [Something]," or "[Something] Frontman"
- Using "Frontman" in a domain name for a commercial product or service
- Using the Frontman logo or a confusingly similar logo in any commercial product

### 4.2 Implied Endorsement

- Using the marks in any way that implies endorsement, sponsorship, or affiliation with Frontman AI
- Presenting your product as an official Frontman product or service

### 4.3 Logo Modifications

- Modifying, distorting, or altering the Frontman logo in any way
- Combining the Frontman logo with other logos or graphics

### 4.4 Artificial Intelligence and Automated Systems

- Using Frontman brand assets (name, logo, marketing materials, documentation) as training data, fine-tuning data, or prompt data for machine learning models or AI systems for the purpose of generating content that appears to originate from or be endorsed by Frontman AI
- Operating any AI chatbot, agent, or automated system that represents itself as "Frontman," "Frontman-powered," or affiliated with Frontman AI without a written license agreement
- Fine-tuning or conditioning AI models on Frontman brand materials to produce outputs that impersonate or simulate Frontman AI communications
- Generating AI content that uses the Frontman name or branding in a way that could cause confusion about its origin

## 5. Forks and Derivative Works

If you fork or create a derivative work of Frontman's open-source code:

- You **must** choose a distinct name for your fork that does not include "Frontman"
- You **may** state that your project is "based on Frontman" or "derived from Frontman" for accurate attribution
- You **must not** use the Frontman logo or any confusingly similar branding
- You **must** remove all Frontman branding from user-facing interfaces in your fork

Examples:
- Acceptable: "AcmeAgent (based on Frontman)"
- Not acceptable: "Frontman Plus," "Frontman Enterprise," "OpenFrontman"

## 6. Commercial License Holders

Entities that hold a valid commercial license agreement with Frontman AI may use the Frontman marks as specified in their agreement. Commercial license holders should refer to their agreement for specific branding rights and restrictions.

## 7. Requesting Permission

For any use not covered by this policy, or to request permission for a prohibited use, contact us at legal@frontman.ai.

## 8. Enforcement

Frontman AI reserves the right to:

- Review and object to any use of our marks that does not comply with this policy
- Revoke permission for any use previously permitted, with reasonable notice
- Take legal action to protect our trademarks from infringement or dilution

## 9. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted to this file in the Frontman repository. Continued use of the marks after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
